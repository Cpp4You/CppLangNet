# Community

## <b>C++</b>

### Discord servers

> [**C++ Language**](https://discord.gg/VRGPw5JCX5) <small><i>(this link is an invite)</i></small>  
CppLangNet's offical discord server. Focuses on CppLangNet development and C++.

> [**#include**](https://www.includecpp.org/discord/)  
`#include <C++>` is a global, inclusive, and diverse community for developers interested in C++.
Here, you can find a welcoming space to learn and discuss C++. We also provide resources to create safer, more inclusive, community gatherings. <small><i>(description taken from the website)</i></small>

> [**Together C & C++**](https://discord.gg/tccpp) <small><i>(this link is an invite)</i></small>  
Server focused around C and C++ and CS/IT.

> [**LLVM**](https://discord.gg/GFK3TmG6EQ) <small><i>(this link is an invite)</i></small>  
LLVM's official discord server. Focused on LLVM tooling (compilers, debuggers, linters, etc.) and C++.

> [**Better C++**](https://discord.com/invite/sJJjX8B) <small><i>(this link is an invite)</i></small>  
A discord server based around (mostly) C++ programming with some C and other languages sprinkled around. We like Rust but C++ is our main focus. Just come and look around. <small><i>(description taken from <a href="https://discadia.com/server/bettercpp/">Discadia</a>)</i></small>

## <b>General IT/CS</b>

:::note
Most of the communities above are also to more general CS/IT topics.

### Discord servers:

> [**The Programmer's Hangout**](https://discord.com/invite/programming) <small><i>(this link is an invite)</i></small> <small><i>(<a href="https://theprogrammershangout.com/">website</a>)</i></small>  
The Programmer's Hangout (TPH) is a discord community geared towards programming.
The use of the word "geared" here is important because more accurately it's a discord for programmers of all kinds.
If you're a green noob with 5 lines of code under your belt, or if you're a veteran with 15 years of industry experience, TPH has a place for you.
More than that, there are over 90,000 people here.
You can guarantee you won't be the only person with your experience level on the server. <small><i>(description taken from <a href="https://theprogrammershangout.com/about">blog's about me</a>)</i></small>
